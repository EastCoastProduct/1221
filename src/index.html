The Company

1221 Capital LLC is a boutique investment firm specializing in trading commodity and financial futures. 1221 Capital offers investment programs designed to deliver consistent, positive results regardless of stock market direction, and put the odds of success firmly on the side of the investor.

1221 Capital LLC is a Boston based investment firm registered with the Commodity Futures Trading Commission and member of the National Futures Association. As Commodity Trading Advisors, we offer individual investors as well as institutional investors the opportunity to diversify their portfolio in an asset class called Managed Futures. 1221 Capital investment programs are offered through Separately Managed Accounts. We happily work individually with investors or alongside their current team of investment advisors. The goal is to identify an investment program which will best support the clients current portfolio and investment goals as a diversification tool. When working with 1221 Capital, clients can expect prompt professional service from a dedicated account manager who will take all the time necessary for the client to have a thorough understanding of their account and investment process.

Investment Approach

1221 Capital Managed Account programs have the ability to profit in both rising as well as declining markets.

A 1221 Capital Managed Account opens the door to a world of opportunity very few investors are aware of, yet one that, sophisticated, institutional investors have participated in for decades. 1221 Capital investment programs have the ability to profit in both rising as well as declining markets and utilize mathematical techniques, with an emphasis on risk versus reward to increase the probability of success for the investor.

Our approach to investing consists mainly of technical analysis, and rules defined investment strategies form the basis of an entry and exit plan for each trade. It is our belief that the cornerstone to a successful investment program is a strict adherence to sound money management principles. 1221 Capital investment programs offer the incredible opportunities provided in the futures markets as well as a liquid diversification solution.

Program One: 1221 Capitals Trend Following Program

  • Dynamic Portfolio Management: Regularly analyze price trends and make appropriate portfolio changes to best position the client for success
  • Use math to increase the odds of profitability
  • Sees no need to be fully invested at all times, seeks only to put money to work when most advantageous

Program Two: 1221 Capitals Option Selling Program

  • Creates a cash flowing asset not dependent upon price appreciation for success
  • Collect option premium upfront for the risk taken
  • Capitalize on the time decay aspect inherent in option pricing

Program Three: 1221 Capitals Equity Index Trading Program

  • Invests in US Equity Index Futures
  • Market timing using technical analysis
  • Investment program will be long, short or cash depending on market outlook
